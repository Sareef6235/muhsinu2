Examination Rules

01

Punctuality

All candidates must be present in the examination hall at least 15 minutes before the scheduled start time.

02

Identification

Students must carry their valid identity card and hall ticket for every examination session.

03

Materials

Only essential stationery items are allowed. Mobile phones, smartwatches, and other electronic devices are strictly prohibited.

04

Integrity

Any form of malpractice or communication between candidates during the exam will lead to immediate disqualification.

For further queries, please visit the Administrative Office.
